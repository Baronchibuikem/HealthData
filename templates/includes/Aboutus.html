our mission

    Our mission is to make data readily available for

  1. Educational and Research purposes
  2. Medical support from NGO's, Government, International Organizations for our users
  3. Collaboration between Health Workers and Patients
  4. Real time monitoring and combating of epidemic outbreaks
Who are we

We are a dedicated team from all over Africa with the shared vision of changing the narrative of the health system in Africa

We are focused on decentralizing the health system in African and provide African people with real access to NGO's, International Organization, Foreign Aids that they need to combat various diseases and have access to people ready to help them
